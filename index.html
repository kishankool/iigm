Welcome To IIGM

Welcome to India's first practice focussed institute in livestock and natural resource Entrepreneurship and Management with a mission to build capable and committed professionals through innovation in imparting education, training and research.

Philosophy Of Entrepreneurship

Building competent professionals for implementations,research and Entrepreneurial initiatives in natural resource management domain.

School Of Practice

Combining traditional wisdom and practices of natural resource management to foster culture of innovation, integration,partnerships and sustainability.

Futuristic Hybrid Learning

Experience the joy of hands on exposure on the ground supported with latest digital tools for perfect learning and doing.

Featured Courses

Livestock Entrepreneurship And Business Management

This is the Flagship course of IIGM. Be employment ready with a 6 months rigorous module on all facets of Livestock Entrepreneurship and Business Management.

Commercial Goat Farming and Management

This course provides you hands on experience from seasoned entrepreneurs, practitioners and focusses on ways to maximize your profits from Day 1.

Livestock Based Livelihoods Project Management (LLPM)

This 3 months course provides strong insights into project management and monitoring and common issues faced and solutions from experienced veterans.

Diploma in Livestock Entrepreneurship and Business Management

In this one year engagement, Learn hands on with maximum field exposure, working with seasoned professionals and entrepreneurs and away from the City Buzz in campus situated in Kukrail Jungle.

Goat Value Chain Management

This six months hybrid program is desgined to prepare aspiring commercial goat farmers or Experienced professionals looking to explore livestock based projects.
